Taylor’s Everyday Granola

★★★★★

Breakfast

Ingredients

Dry:

4 cups rolled oats

1 tsp salt

1 tsp cinnamon

1/2 cup flax seeds

1/2 cup chia seeds

1/2 cup shredded coconut

(1-2 cups of any dry add in will do)


Wet:

1/2 cup melted coconut oil

1/2 cup maple syrup

1 tsp vanilla extract


Directions

- Preheat oven to 350
- Line baking sheet with parchment paper
- In a large bowl, combine oats, salt, and cinnamon
- In a small bowl, combine all wet ingredients
- Pour the wet ingredients over the oats and mix thoroughly until all of the oats are coated
- Add the chia seeds, flax seeds, and shredded coconut to the oat mixture and mix thoroughly until all of the add in’s are sticking to the oats
- Pour the oat mixture onto the baking sheet in one even layer. Using a cutting board or a peice of parchment paper and your hand, press the oat mixture firmly so that it is packed down on the baking sheet
- Bake 20-25 minutes or until golden brown
- Allow to cool on a cooling rack for at least one hour, but preferably several hours
- After the granola is cooled, use a spatula to break the sheet of granola into 8 squares and store in an airtight container for up to 2 weeks
