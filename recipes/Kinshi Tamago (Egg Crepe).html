Kinshi Tamago (Egg Crepe)

Misc.

Ingredients

2 large eggs (50 g each w/o shell) (beaten)

2 tsp sugar

¼ tsp kosher/sea salt (I use Diamond Crystal; Use half for table salt)

neutral-flavored oil (vegetable, canola, etc)

Description

Egg crepe used as a topping for japanese dishes

Directions

For eggs, you make thin egg crepe and cut into thin strips (Kinshi Tamago). If you want to make super-thin crepe, follow my recipe here (just one cookbook)

Whisk together the eggs, sugar, and salt. Heat the oil in the pan over medium heat. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and cook on both sides.

Cool the egg crepe and slice into very thin strips
