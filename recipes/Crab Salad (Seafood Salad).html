Crab Salad (Seafood Salad)

Entrees/Full Meals, Other, Seafood

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes Servings: Yield: 4 servings Source: dinnerthendessert.com

Ingredients

1 pound imitation crab krab meat

1 shallot minced (you can use red onion if you need)

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1/2 cup celery minced

1/2 teaspoon paprika

1/2 teaspoon dill

1/4 teaspoon Kosher salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

Note: click on times in the instructions to start a kitchen timer while cooking.

In a large bowl add all the ingredients together gently, stirring until well coated.

Refrigerate for an hour before serving.

Notes

Try serving on a grilled bun, over pasta or in an avocado
