Thai Peanut Curry and Rice

Asian, Rice, Vegan/Vegetarian

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 20 min Source: Thebellyrulesthemind.net

Ingredients

Stir Fry:

1 medium onion / chopped finely

1/2 red bell pepper/ diced finely

1 tbsp ginger grated

1 tbsp garlic minced

3 tbsp red curry paste


Sauce:

1 + 1/2 cup Coconut milk (almost a full can)

1/4 cup creamy peanut butter

1/4 cup water

1 tbsp soy sauce/ or tamari

1 tbsp sugar

1/2- 1 tsp sriracha

1/2 tsp red pepper flakes / optional

salt to taste


Garnish:

sliced green onions

roasted peanuts


Served with:

2-4 cups cooked rice

1 14oz package Tofu/ extra firm

(prepare tofu via “Crispy Baked Tofu” recipe by Cookie and Kate)

Directions

Cook rice and tofu to taste

In a medium bowl combine peanut butter with hot water and microwave for 30 seconds. Whisk the peanut butter and water until well combined, then whisk in coconut milk, soy sauce, sriracha, and sugar until smooth.

Add oil to a large pan and saute chopped onions, bell pepper, ginger and garlic until softened.

Add in the red curry and coconut peanut sauce mix and stir to combine.

Add in the tofu cubes and bring to a low simmer for 5-10 minutes.

Serve over rice and garnish with chopped roasted peanuts and sliced green onions
