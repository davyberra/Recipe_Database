Coconut Margarita

Drinks

Servings: Servings 2 Source: Foodiecrush.com

Ingredients

1 1/2 ounces Patron reposado tequila

½ ounce cointreau

1 ounce coconut cream

1 ounce coconut milk

1/2 ounce fresh lime juice

Splash of half and half

Splash of simple syrup 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 cup water brought to a boil until sugar dissolved, then cooled.

lime wedges for garnish

Directions

Shake and strain into a glass and serve on the rocks with a lime wedge.
