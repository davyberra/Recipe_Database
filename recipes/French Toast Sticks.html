French Toast Sticks

Breakfast, Desserts, Long

Source: Culinaryhill.com

Ingredients

For the cinnamon sugar topping:

1/2 cup granulated sugar

2 teaspoons cinnamon

For the French toast sticks:

2 cups milk

¼ cup granulated sugar

2 eggs

½ teaspoon Salt

½ teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 teaspoons baking powder

8 slices Texas Toast each cut into 4 strips

2 quarts vegetable oil or any other neutral oil

Directions

To make the cinnamon sugar topping:

In a shallow dish, whisk together cinnamon and sugar. Set aside.

To make the French toast sticks:

Preheat oven to 200 degrees. Line a baking sheet with foil for easy cleanup.

In a large bowl, whisk together milk, sugar, eggs, salt, and vanilla until smooth. Add flour and baking powder, stirring to combine until there are no lumps.

In a large, deep skillet, Dutch oven, or deep fryer, heat oil to 350 degrees. Working in batches (I do 4 sticks a time), dip bread sticks in batter, allowing excess to drip back into the bowl.

Fry until golden brown on one side, about 2 to 3 minutes. Flip each stick and continue to cook until golden brown on the second side sides. Drain on paper towels and immediately roll in cinnamon sugar topping.

Remove to prepared baking sheet and keep warm in the oven until serving time. Repeat with remaining sticks.

Notes

To freeze the French Toast sticks:

Cool to room temperature.

Transfer to a small baking sheet or plate and freeze individually until solid, 20 to 30 minutes.

Transfer to a freezer-safe plastic bag.

To reheat frozen French Toast sticks:

Bake straight from the freezer in a 400-degree oven for 7 to 10 minutes.

Microwave straight from the freezer for 1 minute per French Toast stick.
