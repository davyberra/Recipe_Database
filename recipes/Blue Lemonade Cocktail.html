Blue Lemonade Cocktail

Drinks

Source: Cookingwithcurls.com

Ingredients

4 ounces UV Blue Vodka

4 ounces fresh lemonade I used Newman's Own

ice

sliced lemons to garnish

Directions

Fill a 12 ounce glass about ¾ of the way full with ice cubes.

Pour in the vodka.

Pour in the lemonade.

Stir to combine.

Garnish with lemon slices and serve.

Notes

As you can see, this drink is equal parts vodka and lemonade. You can make this drink as small or large as you would like to fit the size of glass you are using.
