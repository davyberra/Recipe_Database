White Chicken Chili

Soups

Ingredients

2 large chicken breasts

4 can great northwestern beans

1 can black beans

1 can corn

2 cans diced mild green chilis

1 small can mild green enchilada sauce (1-3 cups)

1 Quart water

4-5 tsp chicken bouillion

1-2 tsp chili powder

1 tsp taco seasoning+ more for chicken breasts

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp cumin

1/8th tsp white pepper

salt to taste




Top with:

sour cream

shredded cheese

corn chips

cilantro

Directions

-Bake chicken with taco seasoning and shred
-Drain all canned ingredients and add to crock pot, leave out enchilada sauce
-Add 1 quart of water and all sessonings
-Add 1 cup enchilada sauce
-Turn crock pot to medium and bring to a simmer
-Simmer on medium or low for at least 1 hour, up to four hours
-Add shreedded chicken once it is cooked
-Add additional enchilada sauce as needed
