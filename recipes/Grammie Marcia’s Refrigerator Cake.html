Grammie Marcia’s Refrigerator Cake

★★★★★

Desserts, Long

Ingredients

1 4oz pack of vanilla pudding mix

1 8oz container cool whip (room temp)

1/4 cup powdered sugar

1 cup very cold milk


1 box chocolate cake mix

Description

MUST BE MADE 2-3 DAYS AHEAD, requires two round cake pans and one cake stand with lid

Directions

Cake:
Make two round cakes with the cake mix, allow to cool and then refrigerate covered at least 3 hours before frosting.

Frosting:
- set out cool whip for an hour or two
- combine pudding mix, sugar and milk; beat for one minutes (until soft peaks form)
- let sit for one minute (allow jello to set to hard peaks)
- fold in cool whip


When both the cakes and the frosting are chilled, frost the top of one cake and rest the second cake on top of that on the cake stand.

Frost the outside of the cake liberally

Cover tightly and refrigerate for at least two days, up to three days in advance.

keep cake cool at all times
